Biography

UPPERROOM music is a Church group devouted to their religious beleifs in Christ Jesus. They provide spontaneous music every morning, noon, and night. They love to give back to their communities and centers every activity around loving others. They constantly provide a vareity of songs that are uplifting, encouraging, and full of heart. They conside themselves a community of beleivers that value and pursue to mannifest the presence of God through prayer and worship. Their music tends to be a reflection of their heart and longing for Christ and provide these songs as resources to other local churches.
